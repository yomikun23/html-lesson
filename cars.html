HOW IT STARTED

I’d been trying to start this brand for years, but every name I came up with felt forced, fake, or already taken. I was stuck. Then one random night I’m kicking it with my friends, same vibes as always, laughing, talking mess. Somebody cracked a joke so hard the whole room started dying and one of them goes, “Bro, that’s LOL… Laughing Out Loud, you know how they shorten it.” A couple minutes later, somebody else said something wild, and without thinking I just blurted, “No lies told.” The second it left my mouth I said it again slower: “No… Lies… Told.” Then I threw the acronym on it: “NLT.” Everybody started repeating it, throwing it back and forth like it had always existed. By the end of the night we weren’t even having the same conversation anymore; we were just finding new ways to slip “No Lies Told” into everything. That’s when it hit me: that’s the name. Not something I sat down and brainstormed for months. It came straight out of a real moment, with real people, no filter, no cap. That’s what this brand is always going to be: real recognition of real moments. When something is undeniable, you don’t need a paragraph. You just say it how it is: No Lies Told.
