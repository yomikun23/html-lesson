Biography

Pa. Francis Ibukun Igbekoyi

Pa. Francis Ibukun Igbekoyi was born into the noble family of the late Pa. Solomon Igbekoyi Omoniyi, a renowned blacksmith, and Princess Alice Mobolaji Omoniyi (née Adepehin) of Ojomo Lemikan Quarter, Ode-Irele, on the 28th of November, 1928. He was the first child of his parents and was raised in an environment that valued hard work, discipline, and integrity. Pa. F. I. Igbekoyi began his formal education at St. Peter’s Catholic Primary School Ode-irele in 1945, where he obtained the First School Leaving Certificate in 1953. Shortly after, he began his teaching career at Salvation Primary School, Ode-Irele, in 1954. After teaching for a year, his passion for learning and professional development led him to take the entrance examination into the Okitipupa Divisional Teachers’ Training College, Ode-Aye, where he trained for two years and earned the Grade III Teachers’ Certificate. In 1957, as a qualified teacher, he began his teaching career with the Okitipupa Divisional Council. He was first posted to Local Authority Primary School, Mahin, in Ilaje Local Government Area. His dedication and strong leadership qualities soon earned him a transfer to Salawa, where he served as Assistant Headmaster, and later to L.A. School, Akotogbo, as Headmaster in 1959. Always eager to expand his knowledge, he attended Apostolic Teachers’ Training College, Ilesha, in January 1960, where he obtained the Grade II Teachers’ Certificate. Upon completing his training, he returned to the Okitipupa Divisional Council as a Grade II Teacher. In January 1962, Pa. F. I. Igbekoyi was appointed Headmaster at Local Authority Primary School, Ajagba. Determined to further his education, he successfully passed the General Certificate of Education (G.C.E.) Ordinary Level in 1968. This achievement led to his transfer to L.A. Secondary Modern School, Okitipupa, where he continued to serve diligently. During this time, he also served as Headmaster at L.A. Primary School, Ode-Irele. After passing the G.C.E. Advanced Level in 1965, he joined Manuwa Memorial Grammar School, Iju-Odo, as a Tutor, where he continued to shape young minds with passion and discipline. In 1966, Pa. Igbekoyi gained admission into the University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife), where he obtained a Bachelor’s Degree in Education (Geography) in 1969. read more
